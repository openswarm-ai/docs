The primary way to use an App is to open it as a live card on the **workspace canvas** — right alongside your agents and browsers. Apps render as interactive, resizable windows that sit in your workspace and update in real time.

---

## Opening an App on the dashboard

1. On any dashboard, click the **Add App** button in the toolbar (the grid icon), or press `⌘M`
2. An app picker opens with a search bar and a thumbnail preview of every App in your library
3. Click an App to place it on the canvas

The App appears as a floating card and is immediately live.

<Tip>
  You can have multiple App cards open at once, and mix them freely with agent cards and browser cards on the same canvas.
</Tip>

---

## The App card on the dashboard

Once an App is placed on the canvas, it behaves like any other card:

### Moving and resizing

- **Drag** the header bar to reposition the card anywhere on the canvas
- **Resize** by dragging any of the 8 handles on the card's edges and corners
- Position and size are saved automatically per dashboard

### Header controls

| Control | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Refresh** (↺) | Reload the App's iframe — useful if the preview gets into a bad state |
| **✕ Remove** | Removes the App card from the canvas (does not delete the App from your library) |

### Interacting with the App

When the card is **unselected**, the App is fully interactive — click, scroll, type, and use it like any normal web app.

When the card is **selected** (click once to select), an overlay covers the app so you can drag and reposition the card. Click again to deselect and restore full interactivity.

---

## The Apps library

The Apps page (`/apps`) is your library of all saved Apps. Each App is shown as a card with a thumbnail, name, and description. From here you can:

- **Edit** — click the card (or the edit icon on hover) to open the full editor
- **Run** — click the ▶ play icon on hover to open the run dialog
- **Delete** — click the trash icon on hover to permanently remove the App

---

## Removing vs deleting

| Action | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| **Remove from dashboard** (✕ on card header) | Takes the card off the canvas. The App stays in your library. |
| **Delete** (trash icon on library card) | Permanently removes the App from your library. Cannot be undone. |
