Trade real-world outcomes with leverage and rewards

Space is a decentralized prediction market platform built on Solana where you can trade on real-world outcomes across crypto, politics, sports, technology, culture, and more - and earn [**rewards**](https://docs.into.space/features/airdrops) for being right.[**​**](https://docs.into.space/en/start/what-is-space#what-makes-space-different)

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a Market">
    Browse crypto, politics, sports, tech, economics, culture, and more. Pick [**outcomes**](https://docs.into.space/features/categories) you know better than the crowd does.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build Your Position">
    Buy YES or NO shares with [**market orders**](https://docs.into.space/guides/market-order) for instant fills, or set [**limit orders**](https://docs.into.space/guides/limit-order). Cash out anytime or hold until outcome [**resolves**](https://docs.into.space/concepts/resolution).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Multiply Your Returns">
    Multiply your exposure up to 10x with [**leverage**](https://docs.into.space/features/leverage). Small capital and big positions means outsized returns when you’re right.
  </Step>
</Steps>
