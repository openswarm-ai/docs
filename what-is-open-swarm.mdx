Run entire operations with a coordinated swarm of AI agents — and stay in control of every decision that matters.

Open Swarm is a multi-agent orchestration platform where you deploy AI agents across an infinite canvas, each working in parallel on real tasks — research, document drafting, outreach, analysis, and more — while you remain the strategy center directing the whole operation.

## What Makes Open Swarm Different

## How It Works

<Frame>
  ![IMG 1715](/images/IMG_1715.jpeg)
</Frame>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Your Integrations">
    Link the tools your work already lives in — Gmail, Calendar, Drive, GitHub, Slack, and more. Your agents operate across all of them from day one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delegate Tasks">
    Spawn agents and assign them work. Research, outreach, document editing, analysis — each agent takes ownership of its task and runs autonomously while you move on to the next thing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and Direct">
    Agents surface outputs and decisions for your approval. Merge what works, redirect what doesn't, and keep the operation moving — with you in control the whole way.
  </Step>
</Steps>
