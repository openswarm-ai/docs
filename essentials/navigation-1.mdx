The dashboard canvas is infinite and freely navigable. You can zoom in, zoom out, and pan in any direction using your mouse, trackpad, or keyboard.

---

## Panning

### Two-finger scroll (trackpad)

Swipe with two fingers in any direction to pan the canvas. This is the most natural way to move around on a Mac trackpad.

### Middle-click drag

Hold down the **middle mouse button** and drag to pan.

### Right-click drag

Hold down the **right mouse button** and drag to pan.

### Space + drag

Hold **Space** and drag with the left mouse button to pan. The cursor changes to a grab cursor when Space is held.

### ⌘/Ctrl + drag

Hold **⌘** (Mac) or **Ctrl** (Windows/Linux) and drag with the left mouse button to pan.

---

## Zooming

### Pinch-to-zoom (trackpad)

Pinch with two fingers on a trackpad to zoom centered on your cursor position. This maps to the standard "pinch" gesture (`ctrlKey` events from the browser).

### Keyboard shortcuts

| Shortcut | Action |
|---|---|
| **⌘ +** | Zoom in (centered on viewport) |
| **⌘ −** | Zoom out (centered on viewport) |
| **⌘ 0** | Reset zoom to 100% and reset pan to origin |

Zoom range: **15%** to **300%**.

---

## Canvas Controls widget

A small control bar appears in the bottom-right corner of the canvas:

| Control | Description |
|---|---|
| **−** | Zoom out one step |
| **N%** | Current zoom percentage. Click to reset to 100%. |
| **+** | Zoom in one step |
| **⊡ Fit to view** | Fit all cards into the viewport with padding |
| **✦ Tidy layout** | Auto-arrange all cards into a clean grid |

### Fit to view

Automatically zooms and pans so that all cards on the canvas are visible, with comfortable padding around them. This also runs automatically on first load.

### Tidy layout

Rearranges all cards into a structured layout. Useful when the canvas has gotten messy after a lot of agent activity.

---

## Zoom sensitivity

Pinch-to-zoom sensitivity can be adjusted in **Settings → Zoom Sensitivity** (range 1–100, default 50). This scales how aggressively trackpad pinch gestures zoom the canvas.

---

## Focus on a card

Clicking a card in the [Dashboard Header](/dashboard/header) dropdown automatically pans and zooms the canvas to center on that card (animated, at 1:1 zoom).

The card briefly highlights with a glow effect to confirm which one was focused.
