title: "Selection"
description: "Selecting, moving, and copying cards on the dashboard canvas."
---

The dashboard supports multi-card selection, letting you move, copy, or operate on multiple cards at once.

---

## Selecting cards

### Click to select

Click on any card to select it. A visual highlight (ring or glow) appears around selected cards.

Clicking a card that is already selected **deselects** it.

Clicking on empty canvas space deselects all.

### Shift + click

Hold **Shift** and click a card to **add it to the current selection** (or remove it if already selected). This lets you build up a selection one card at a time.

### Marquee select (drag to select)

Click and drag on **empty canvas space** (not on a card) to draw a selection rectangle. Any card that intersects the rectangle is selected when you release.

- The marquee box is drawn in canvas-space, so it works correctly at any zoom level.
- Hold **Shift** while dragging to **toggle** intersected cards — cards already selected are deselected, and unselected cards are added.
- The marquee is drawn with a semi-transparent overlay so you can see what's being captured.

<Note>
  Marquee selection is disabled while **element selection mode** (for attaching page elements as context) is active.
</Note>

### Deselect all

- Click on empty canvas space
- Press **Escape**

---

## Moving multiple cards

When multiple cards are selected, **dragging any one of them** moves the entire selection together.

The drag delta is applied to all selected cards simultaneously — their relative positions are preserved.

Cards that are not selected are not affected.

---

## Copy & paste

Selected cards (agent cards, view cards, and browser cards) can be copied and pasted onto the canvas.

Use standard system shortcuts:
- **⌘C** — copy selected cards to the dashboard clipboard
- **⌘V** — paste the clipboard cards onto the canvas, offset slightly from their original positions

The clipboard persists within the session (not across app restarts).

---

## Keyboard reference

| Key | Action |
|---|---|
| **Click** | Select card / deselect if already selected |
| **Shift + Click** | Add/remove card from selection |
| **Drag on canvas** | Marquee-select |
| **Shift + Drag** | Marquee-toggle |
| **Escape** | Deselect all |
| **⌘C** | Copy selected cards |
| **⌘V** | Paste copied cards |
