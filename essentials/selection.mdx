The dashboard supports multi-card selection, letting you move, copy, or operate on multiple cards at once.

---

## Selecting cards

### Drag to select

Click and drag on **empty canvas space** (not on a card) to draw a selection rectangle. Any card that intersects the rectangle is selected when you release.

- The selection box is drawn with a semi-transparent overlay so you can see what's being captured.
- If you've already selected some cards, you can hold **Shift** while dragging to select more cards — cards already selected are deselected, and unselected cards are added.

<Note>
  When we say "Drag to select", this is different than **element selection mode** which lets you add context for agents. "Drag to select" refers to the default selection which helps you move, copy, and delete cards in a workspace.
</Note>

### Deselect all

- Click on empty canvas space
- Press **Escape**

---

## Moving multiple cards

When multiple cards are selected, **dragging any one of them** moves the entire selection together.

The drag delta is applied to all selected cards simultaneously — their relative positions are preserved.

Cards that are not selected are not affected.

---

## Copy & paste

Selected cards (agent cards, view cards, and browser cards) can be copied and pasted onto the canvas.

Use standard system shortcuts:
- **⌘C** — copy selected cards to the dashboard clipboard
- **⌘V** — paste the clipboard cards onto the canvas, offset slightly from their original positions

---

## Keyboard reference

| Key | Action |
|---|---|
| **Shift + Click** | Add/remove card from selection |
| **Drag on canvas** | Marquee-select |
| **Shift + Drag** | Marquee-toggle |
| **Escape** | Deselect all |
| **⌘C** | Copy selected cards |
| **⌘V** | Paste copied cards |
